## Cole's Pancake Recipe
<!---
Put your name or github username somewhere
-->



### Ingredients (serves 2)
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tbsp butter, melted


### Instructions
1. mix dry ingredients
2. mix in wet ingredients into same bowl
3. cook on griddle
4. enjoy!
