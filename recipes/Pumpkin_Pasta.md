## Lucy's Pumpkin Pasta

Perfect for fall! 

### Ingredients
- 1 can pumpkin puree
- 4 cups spinach
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- Parmesean cheese
- Pasta 
- 3 cloves garlic
- Butter 

### Instrucitons 
1. Make the pasta and drain
2. Saute spinach in garlic and butter
3. Mix in puree and cream
4. Add cheese
5. Pour over pasta
